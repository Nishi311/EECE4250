Database Categories:

walkability: index score, 0 to 100, 100 being best
transit: index score, 0 to 100, 100 being best
pop_density: people/sq mile
bikeability: index score, 0 to 100, 100 being best
metro_population: total population of city and surounding areas
prop_crime: number of annual crimes/100,000 residents
violent_crime: number of annual crimes/100,000 residents
air_pollution: index score, 0 to 100, 100 being worst
traffic: index score, 0 to 100, 100 being worst
sunshine: annual sunshine hours
